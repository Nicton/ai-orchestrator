# Glossary of abbreviations (from MA-2026-05-25 test cases)

Abbreviations were extracted primarily from test titles/descriptions and then expanded when the surrounding text made the meaning reasonably inferable.

## Confirmed / strongly inferable
- **TMS** — Transport Management System
- **CSW** — *Create Shipment Wizard* (in tests: “Pick-up date on … CSW”, “mandatory fields of CSW”)
- **SR** — Shipment Request (in tests: “Creating of SR”, “Auto-confirm SR”)
- **QR** — Quote Request (in tests: “Creating of QR”, “Auto-confirm QR”, “Waiting quote status”)
- **RTB** — Ready To Book (in tests: “Creating Ready to book”, “RTB option/filter”)
- **POD** — Proof Of Delivery (referenced directly as POD)
- **PDF** — Portable Document Format
- **ETA** — Estimated Time of Arrival (referenced directly as ETA timing)
- **UTC** — Coordinated Universal Time
- **LOCODE** — UN/LOCODE (location code standard; referenced as LOCODE)
- **FTL** — Full Truck Load
- **LTL** — Less Than Truck Load
- **FCL** — Full Container Load
- **CO2** — Carbon dioxide (emissions; appears as CO2)
- **KG** — kilogram
- **RDV** — Rendez-vous / appointment (common FR abbreviation; appears alongside EN/FR/locale-related items)

## Likely / inferred but not explicit in text
- **PML** — *Public Master Location* or *Product Master Location* (tests explicitly mention “Public Master Location (PML) user”; “Master Location” appears together with PML)
- **SH** — Shipment (appears frequently next to booking/tracking context)
- **BK / BOOKING** — Booking (BK appears frequently; BOOKING appears as a token too)

## Unknown (seen in test-case text, expansion not reliable from context alone)
- **ML** — unknown (possibly *Milestone List* / *Milestones*)
- **TP** — unknown (possibly *Transport Plan*)
- **PSH** — unknown
- **FU** — unknown (appears in spectator-related tests: “FU - keys are not displayed from spectators”)
- **POL** — unknown/ambiguous (commonly *Port of Loading* in ocean freight; confirm in product context)
- **MA** — unknown (could be “Main app”/project prefix; appears as a token in text)
- **AE** — unknown
- **RS** — unknown
